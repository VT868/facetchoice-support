Privacy Policy

Effective July 10, 2026

Data collection

FacetChoice does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. Decisions and settings are stored locally on the user’s device.

Permissions and third parties

The app does not request access to location, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, health data, tracking, or advertising identifiers. It contains no analytics, advertising SDKs, user accounts, or cloud service.

Retention and deletion

User entries remain on the device until the user deletes them or uninstalls the app. Sharing a summary uses Apple’s system share sheet and occurs only when the user explicitly chooses it. The selected destination may have its own privacy practices.

There is no server-side user data to access, correct, revoke, or delete. Users can remove all local data by deleting entries or uninstalling FacetChoice.

Changes

If FacetChoice’s privacy practices change, this policy and the App Store privacy disclosure will be updated before the change takes effect.

Contact

For privacy questions, open a support request. Please do not include private or sensitive information.
