How can we help?

FacetChoice is an offline decision-comparison tool for iPhone. Your decisions stay on your device.

  • Create a decision: choose a template, add options, then adjust criteria and weights.
  • Understand a result: open any decision to see its ranking and score chart.
  • Share: use Share Summary to export a text recap through the iOS share sheet.
  • Delete local data: delete entries in the app or uninstall FacetChoice to remove all app data.

Contact support

For help, bug reports, or privacy questions, open a support request on GitHub. Please do not include private or sensitive information.
